less confused, more natural and as seamless ad possible.Despite the vast increase and evolution of the ways we interact with our mobile devices over the past yeas, the basic principle has remained the same, resulting in using only the on screen position of the fingers touching the screen.We propose Fat Finger, that will additionally make use of the contact size of the finger touching the screen.We developed an application in the form of target selection tasks and performed a user study where users had to succwssfully go through a series of different target selection trials.\cleardoublepage%Dedication\chapter*{}\Large\vspace{5cm}\emph{To family, friends and the art of dance...}\cleardoublepage\normalsize\chapter*{Acknowledgements}As the saying
